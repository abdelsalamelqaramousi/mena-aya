carent

carent It is an automotive-focused e-commerce marketplace that brings innovation to the new car market, where customers can buy and sell cars reliably and transparently. The company aims to build confidence in the automobile market by becoming the preferred place for customers seeking to buy and sell their cars, through a wide range of high-quality vehicles that can be accessed through a variety of financing solutions.
